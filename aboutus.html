About Us

Slums going green and clean (SGGC) is a community based organization founded in 2017(registered in 2018) we work to improve the living condition in informal settlement such as slums by promoting environmental sustainability and cleanliness the organization activity include:

Our Aim
  • Is To create awareness about eco friendly community in the informal settlements,empower the community through skilled based programs.
  • Solid waste management: SGGC work to improve waste management in the slums by promoting recycling, composting and other waste reduction services.
  • We establish recycling centers for the collection, sorting, and processing of recyclable materials..
  • We implement composting facilities for organic waste management and soil enrichment.
  • We initiated Black Soldier Fly (BSF) farming as a sustainable method for organic waste conversion and animal feed production.
  • We empower youth and women through training, skill development, and entrepreneurship opportunities in waste management and sustainable agriculture.
  • We promote environmental awareness and community participation in waste reduction and recycling initiatives. .

Community Clean-up Campaigns.

Waste Segregation and Recycling

We do Conduct interactive workshops and training sessions to educate community members about the benefits of waste segregation and recycling. We do Teach residents how to properly separate recyclable materials (such as plastics, paper, glass, and metals) from non-recyclable waste. We Demonstrate simple recycling techniques that can be implemented at the household level, such as composting organic waste or repurposing materials for crafts.


February 20, 2024 view more

Training Programs for Waste Pickers

We do Organize training workshops to enhance the skills and knowledge of informal waste pickers operating within the slum community. We Offer training on waste sorting, material recovery techniques, occupational safety, and entrepreneurship skills. We Facilitate access to resources such as protective gear, sorting equipment, and market linkages to improve the efficiency and livelihoods of waste pickers

Beautification Projects

We Engage community members in beautification projects aimed at enhancing the visual appeal and livability of the slum environment. We Collaborate with local artists, schools, and youth groups to create murals, sculptures, and other artworks that reflect community pride and cultural identity. We Undertake landscaping initiatives to green public spaces, plant trees, and flowers, and install benches or recreational facilities for residents to enjoy.

Capacity Building Workshops

we Offer a series of capacity-building workshops and training sessions focused on environmental conservation, sustainable living practices, and community leadership. We Cover topics such as waste management, water conservation, renewable energy, climate change adaptation, and disaster preparedness. We Empower residents with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to actively participate in decision-making processes and take ownership of local sustainability initiatives.

Partnerships with Local Authorities and Businesses

We Establish collaborative partnerships with local government agencies, businesses, and civil society organizations to leverage resources, expertise, and support for green and clean initiatives.
